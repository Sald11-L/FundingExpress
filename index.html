Bank Statement Disclosure & Consent

By uploading your business bank statements to FundingExpressAi, you acknowledge and agree to the following:

1. Purpose of Collection

Your bank statements are collected solely to verify your business revenue, cash flow, and banking history for the purpose of generating a funding estimate and, if you proceed, evaluating your application with our funding partners and lenders.

2. How Your Information Is Used

Statements may be reviewed by FundingExpressAi and shared with third-party lenders, underwriters, or funding partners solely for purposes of assessing your eligibility for funding. Statements will not be used for any purpose unrelated to your application without your separate consent.

3. Data Security

Uploaded documents are transmitted and stored using industry-standard encryption and bank-level security controls. Access is limited to personnel and partners directly involved in underwriting your application.

4. Accuracy

You represent that the bank statements you upload are true, accurate, and unaltered copies of your actual business account records. Submitting falsified, altered, or misrepresented statements may result in denial of your application, rescission of any funding offer, and may constitute fraud under applicable law.

5. No Guarantee of Approval

Submission of bank statements does not guarantee approval, a specific funding amount, or specific terms. All funding estimates are educational and AI-generated; final offers are subject to lender review of your full application and supporting documents.

6. Retention

Bank statements will be retained only as long as necessary to process your application and comply with recordkeeping obligations, after which they will be securely deleted or archived per our data retention policy, as described in our Privacy Policy & Terms of Service.

7. Consent

By checking the agreement box and selecting “Get my estimate,” you consent to FundingExpressAi and its funding partners collecting, storing, and using your bank statement data as described above and in our full Privacy Policy & Terms of Service.
